Kitchen remodel myths vs. facts

Myth: Quartz countertops are indestructible.

Fact: Quartz can scorch above 300°F and stain from prolonged contact with turmeric, red wine, or permanent marker.

Myth: Stock cabinets always cost less than semi-custom.

Fact: Once filler strips, modifications, and accessories are added, stock projects often land within 10–15% of semi-custom totals.

Myth: You don't need a permit for a "cosmetic" kitchen remodel in Cobb County.

Fact: Any electrical, local services, or gas line modification requires a permit under Cobb County Code of Ordinances Chapter 6.

Myth: All granite needs annual sealing.

Fact: Modern resin-treated granite often holds a seal 3–5 years; test by dropping water — if it beads, no resealing needed.
